Hueco Tiempo

October 17, 2023
TouchDesigner, for Projection
Projected to 30’ by 10’ wall

Brandon Art370 assignment to design a "visual clock" that displays a novel or unconventional representation of the time. Appears to be different shades depending on if the sun is up or down. Pendulum swings in time to seconds, center sun pulses and sends out a ripple on the minute.
