Frogs and Biodiversity

March 2, 2024

During a week-long data science challenege hosted by the UMD Info School, I worked in a small team to build a Species Distribution Model for the Litoria Fallax, the Eastern Dwarf Tree Frog, a wonderful indicator species for ecological health. By overlaying the frog occurence data with Terraclimate collections, a weather database, our model was able to predict the presence of the frogs given geo and ecological conditions with 80% accuracy. Delivered via Canva slides, we WON best group presentation!
Peers: Sweta Dantu, John Hampton III
