Charcoal Forest

Sept - Dec 2024
Created the following demo in the Fall 2024 semester in IMDM490, Capstone I.
A VR experience in which users traverse a map of forest trails by moving onto lantern panels. Will be coming work into Spring 2025, incorporating charcoal drawings in the 3D VR environment
and leveraging an in-game controller-bound lantern for pathing decisions.
